About me

I am curious and analytic person who will take on any challenge in front of me with a drive to develop, learn and innovate that borders on obsession. I was most recently in a dual role combining Print Coordination and Project Management at IKEA of Sweden. I am at my best when I have a difficult task to accomplish. I am driven, smart, technical and ridiculously persistent. I learn fast and enjoy working to gather information and develop rather than being too specifically instructed – what I don´t know I can find out and what skills I lack I can acquire, that is my attitude and it´s what I most enjoy doing. Doing it together with a team is even better!

The idea is that this page will be a presentation of me, a form of practice and a way to market myself.
It´s my intention to let it grow as my skills and knowledge improve - and who knows - it might be a bit of fun too.
